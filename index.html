Case: Rebranding a Jewelry Store

The Source is a sustainable jewelry store, based in Copenhagen, which aims to make more from less, and be enviornmetnal friendly at the same time. Our mission was to rebuild their brand image based on their values and vision. It had to be raw but refreshing. Elegant and simple but not your typical jewelry store.

Our concept is, itself, pretty "raw". We decided to combine nature with jewelry, to send the client to "The Source" of the product. When they wear our products, they start a journey, back to the origins to say so. They raw materials are strong, they inspire confidence, and they can only born the jewelry by combining the forces, which a call for a community.

The design of the jewelry does not follow conventional patters, and neither did we. We built graphic elements inspired by wood sections to reflet the imperfect design of the collection and enhance the beauty, while keeping it elegant.

We decided to build a more lively and interactive website with a stronger contrast, which is why we used a lot of dark tones. We, however, balanced the design with a lot of white space. The graphics create good dynamics and the images build a little mystery, which make people want to explore some more. Every section has a call to action, every page has a different story, while keeping the design consistent.

We built a new social media strategy, with a consistent posting algorithm, where we have ads as stories that promote the collection. Both on stories and feed we have "teaser" videos for the campaign, and, eventually, the main video. The purpose of posting on a regular basis is to increase the online community and spread the word. The main video was built with a short storyline and a clifhanger, so we keep our current clients interested and curious, and attract new ones.
