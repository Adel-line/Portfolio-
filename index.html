Okay, really, but who I am?

I shoot because I see, I see beauty, wander, joy and excitment. I see things that need to be shared and remembered. Then, if I don’t do it, I don’t know who will. Photography is a universal language and can be easily understood, no matter if it's about people, landscapes, buildings, moments, feelings, heartbeats. I shoot because it gives me the power to stop time and capture moments to indulge in later. My ultimate feeling of success is drawing other people into those moments as well.
