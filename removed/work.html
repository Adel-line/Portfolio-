The Source

Sustainability for Longevivity

Collaboration with local start-up.

The Source is a sustainable jewelry store, based in Copenhagen, which aims to make more from less, and be enviornmetnal friendly at the same time. Our mission was to rebuild their brand image based on their values and vision. It had to be raw but refreshing. Elegant but not your typical jewelry store.

We created this "raw concept" based on natural resources (wood, stone), where we draw inspiration for the background shapes on the website as well as the storyline for the video. I handlded a big part of the visual production, from shooting and editing the videos production to arranging the set for the prodcut photos.

We built a new social media strategy, will regular stories that will advertise the collection as well as "teaser" videos posted on the IG feed, to attract new customers and keep the existing ones interested and curious.

Technologies Used: Adobe Suite, Github, HTML/CSS, JavaScript
